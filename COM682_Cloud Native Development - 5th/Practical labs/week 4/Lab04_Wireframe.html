Teaching Notes & Student Activities

Wireframing Principles to Teach:

  • Focus on layout and structure, not visual design
  • Consider user flow and navigation
  • Plan for different screen sizes (responsive design)
  • Use consistent spacing and alignment
  • Prioritize content hierarchy

Student Exercises:

  1. Sketch a wireframe for a personal portfolio website
  2. Create a wireframe for a local business website
  3. Redesign the navigation for mobile devices
  4. Plan a website with a different layout (one-page vs multi-page)
  5. Add annotations explaining design decisions

Discussion Questions:

  • Why is wireframing important before coding?
  • How does wireframing help with client communication?
  • What elements are essential in every website wireframe?
  • How do you decide what content to prioritize?
  • What are the challenges of mobile-first wireframing?
